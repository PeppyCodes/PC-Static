Research

Research Interest

Thermoelectric Materials

  • Understanding of Transport properties measurements (Seebeck coefficient, electrical conductivity, Thermal conductivity)
  • Correlation studies on Structure- Chemistry with thermoelectric properties
  • Band tuning and engineering via process design and stoichiometric optimisation
  • Nano structure thermoelectrics for high performance
  • Synergetic enhancement of thermoelectric properties via Complex chemistry design (High Entropy phenomena and multi-phase)
  • Different scale effects (Meso, micro and nano) in thermoelectric properties

Design of Thermoelectric Generator

  • Mechanical properties of thermoelectric materials
  • Diffusion joining of thermoelectric materials with substrate
  • Design and assembling prospectus of TEG/TEM

Advanced Materials Processing

    Spark plasma sintering

  • Spark plasma sintering of materials (thermoelectric, composite, High entropy alloy, dissimilar material joining, Functional Graded Materials etc.,)
  • Spark Plasma assisted combustion synthesis or self-propagating high temperature synthesis approaches
  • Densification mechanisms

    • Non-equilibrium and Nano materials synthesis by Mechanical milling/alloying

Evaluation of Mechanical properties

  • Hardness evaluation
  • Fracture toughness materials through indentation method
  • Structure- properties correlation
