My Research Areas

Light weight alloy development

To design and optimize the chemical compositions of magnesium-lithium alloys in order to obtain either single phase or two phases. The systems chosen for investigation will have a base composition of the Mg-Li system,and alloying additions that will be considered include Ca and Al.

Aluminium and Magnesium based composites.

To optimize of rolling / ECAP parameters to understand the workability of single phase and two phase Mg-Li alloys without and with ternary additions.

Extrusion, Rolling and ECAP

To understand the microstructural evolution and texture development by scanning electron microscopy (SEM) and X-ray texture analysis etc. From the results, the effect of alloying elements on the development of microstructure will be understood.

Mechanical Behaviour of Materials.

To simulate the texture evolution as a result of the two deformation processes.

Heat treatment of Non-Ferrous alloys.

To carry out relevant mechanical property (Tensile, Hardness) evaluation corresponding to different combinations of texture and microstructure. Efforts will be made to understand the effects of solute elements on structure and properties.

Tribology.

To conduct thermal treatments at an appropriate temperature, time and atmosphere in order to study the phase transformation. Studies on thermal stability include, determination of the role of alloying additions on the thermal stability of the selected base alloy and the factors influencing the structure and properties of alloys.
