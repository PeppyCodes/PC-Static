About GEMM

GEMM Research Group – An overview

Our Green Energy Materials and Manufacturing (GEMM) Research Group is the functional research group in the Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli, India. This has created an ideal environment for carrying out leading edge research by integrating modern experimental and computational technologies. The GEMM research group stands on the mission on the development and understanding the science behind the functional phenomena of materials for energy and its related applications through the manufacturing process through the green approach thus ensuring the eco-friendly system development.
